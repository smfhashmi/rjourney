# Transition Services

Life is full of transitions, and each one brings both opportunities and challenges. At R Journey, we provide comprehensive support to help individuals with autism and special needs navigate transitions successfully at every stage of life.

## Our Approach to Transitions

We recognize that transitions can be particularly challenging for individuals with autism and special needs. Our services focus on:

- Early planning and preparation
- Individualized support
- Skill development
- Emotional support
- Ongoing follow-up

## Early Childhood Transitions

### Home to Early Intervention

Supporting the transition to early intervention services:

- Understanding early intervention
- Connecting with services
- Supporting families
- Coordinating services
- Monitoring progress

### Early Intervention to Preschool

Preparing for preschool:

- School readiness skills
- Social skill development
- Communication preparation
- Family preparation
- School connections

## School Transitions

### Preschool to Elementary School

Supporting the transition to elementary school:

- School readiness assessment
- Skill development
- School orientation
- Teacher and staff education
- Family support

### Elementary to Middle School

Navigating the middle school transition:

- Academic preparation
- Social skill development
- Independence building
- Self-advocacy skills
- Peer relationships

### Middle to High School

Preparing for high school:

- Academic planning
- Course selection
- Extracurricular involvement
- Career exploration
- Transition planning

### High School to Post-Secondary

Planning for life after high school:

- Post-secondary education options
- Career exploration
- Independent living skills
- Employment preparation
- Adult service connections

## Adulthood Transitions

### Transition to Adult Services

Moving from school-based to adult services:

- Understanding adult service systems
- Eligibility and applications
- Service coordination
- Building adult relationships
- Developing independence

### Employment Transitions

Entering the workforce:

- Career exploration
- Job skills development
- Job search support
- Workplace preparation
- Employment support

### Independent Living Transitions

Moving toward greater independence:

- Living skills assessment
- Skill development
- Housing options
- Support services
- Safety planning

## Family Transitions

### Supporting Families Through Transitions

We recognize that transitions affect entire families:

- Family education and preparation
- Emotional support
- Resource connections
- Planning support
- Ongoing assistance

## Transition Planning

### Individualized Transition Plans

We help develop comprehensive transition plans that include:

- Assessment of current skills and needs
- Identification of goals and preferences
- Development of action steps
- Timeline and milestones
- Support strategies
- Resource connections

### Person-Centered Planning

Using person-centered approaches:

- Focusing on individual goals and dreams
- Involving the individual in planning
- Building on strengths
- Creating support networks
- Developing action plans

## Skill Development for Transitions

### Life Skills

Essential skills for successful transitions:

- Daily living skills
- Money management
- Time management
- Transportation
- Health and wellness
- Safety skills

### Social Skills

Navigating new social environments:

- Making new friends
- Understanding new social expectations
- Building relationships
- Managing social situations
- Self-advocacy

### Academic and Career Skills

Preparing for educational and career transitions:

- Study skills
- Work skills
- Technology skills
- Communication skills
- Problem-solving

## Support During Transitions

### Pre-Transition Support

Before the transition:

- Planning and preparation
- Skill development
- Information and education
- Resource connections
- Anxiety management

### During Transition Support

Active support during the transition:

- On-site support
- Problem-solving
- Adjustment support
- Communication facilitation
- Crisis intervention

### Post-Transition Support

After the transition:

- Follow-up support
- Ongoing assistance
- Problem-solving
- Skill refinement
- Celebration of success

## Success Stories

Our transition services have helped individuals:

- Successfully move to new schools
- Transition to adulthood
- Enter the workforce
- Move toward independence
- Build confidence and skills
- Achieve their goals
