# Autism Resources

Navigating life with autism can be complex, but you don't have to do it alone. This comprehensive resource guide provides information, tools, and connections to help individuals with autism and their families on their journey.

## Understanding Autism

### What is Autism Spectrum Disorder?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental condition characterized by:

- Differences in social communication and interaction
- Restricted, repetitive patterns of behavior, interests, or activities
- Symptoms that appear in early childhood
- A wide spectrum of strengths and challenges

### Key Facts About Autism

- Autism affects approximately 1 in 36 children
- It occurs across all racial, ethnic, and socioeconomic groups
- Boys are diagnosed about 4 times more often than girls
- Early intervention can make a significant difference
- Many individuals with autism have unique strengths and talents
