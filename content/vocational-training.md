# Vocational Training and Employment Support

Meaningful employment is a key component of independence and fulfillment. At R Journey, we provide comprehensive vocational training and employment support to help individuals with autism and special needs build successful careers.

## Our Approach to Vocational Training

We believe that everyone has talents and abilities that can contribute to the workforce. Our programs focus on:

- Identifying individual strengths and interests
- Developing job-specific skills
- Building workplace social skills
- Creating pathways to employment
- Supporting long-term career success

## Skills Assessment and Career Exploration

### Interest and Aptitude Assessments

We help individuals discover:

- Career interests and preferences
- Natural talents and abilities
- Learning styles and work preferences
- Values and work environment preferences
- Career goals and aspirations

### Career Exploration Activities

- Job shadowing opportunities
- Informational interviews
- Workplace tours
- Career fairs and events
- Internship experiences

## Job Skills Training

### Hard Skills Development

Training in specific job-related skills:

- Computer and technology skills
- Industry-specific technical skills
- Safety training and certifications
- Quality standards and procedures
- Equipment operation

### Soft Skills Development

Essential workplace skills:

- Communication in the workplace
- Teamwork and collaboration
- Problem-solving
- Time management
- Professionalism
- Following instructions
- Asking for help when needed

## Workplace Social Skills

Navigating workplace relationships:

- Interacting with supervisors
- Working with colleagues
- Customer service skills
- Handling feedback
- Managing workplace stress
- Understanding workplace culture

## Job Search Support

We assist with:

- Resume and cover letter development
- Job application completion
- Interview preparation and practice
- Portfolio development
- Online job search strategies
- Networking opportunities

## Job Placement Services

Our placement services include:

- Job matching based on skills and interests
- Employer partnerships and connections
- Supported employment services
- Job coaching and on-the-job support
- Follow-up and retention support

## On-the-Job Support

### Job Coaching

Our job coaches provide:

- Initial training and orientation support
- Ongoing support as needed
- Problem-solving assistance
- Skill refinement
- Advocacy with employers
- Transition to independence

### Workplace Accommodations

We help identify and implement:

- Reasonable accommodations
- Assistive technology
- Modified work schedules
- Environmental modifications
- Communication supports

## Supported Employment

For individuals who need ongoing support:

- Long-term job coaching
- Regular check-ins and support
- Crisis intervention
- Career advancement support
- Job retention services

## Entrepreneurship Support

For those interested in starting their own business:

- Business planning support
- Skill development
- Resource connections
- Mentorship opportunities
- Networking support

## Employer Partnerships

We work with employers to:

- Create inclusive workplaces
- Provide training on working with individuals with special needs
- Develop job opportunities
- Support successful employment relationships
- Build long-term partnerships

## Success Stories

Our vocational programs have helped individuals:

- Find meaningful employment
- Develop confidence in their abilities
- Build financial independence
- Create career pathways
- Achieve long-term job success
