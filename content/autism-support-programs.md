# Autism Support Programs

At R Journey, we understand that every individual with autism is unique, with their own strengths, challenges, and goals. Our comprehensive autism support programs are designed to meet individuals where they are and help them progress on their journey toward independence and fulfillment.

## Social Skills Development

Our social skills programs help individuals with autism develop meaningful relationships and navigate social situations with confidence. Through structured activities, role-playing, and peer interactions, participants learn:

- Communication skills
- Emotional regulation
- Social cues and body language
- Friendship building
- Conflict resolution

## Behavioral Support Services

We provide evidence-based behavioral support to help individuals manage challenging behaviors and develop positive alternatives. Our approach includes:

- Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) principles
- Positive behavior support plans
- Functional behavior assessments
- Individualized intervention strategies

## Communication Enhancement

Communication is fundamental to connection and independence. Our programs focus on:

- Verbal and non-verbal communication
- Augmentative and alternative communication (AAC)
- Social communication skills
- Conversation skills
- Self-advocacy

## Sensory Integration

Many individuals with autism experience sensory processing differences. Our sensory integration programs help participants:

- Understand their sensory needs
- Develop coping strategies
- Participate in sensory-friendly activities
- Create sensory-safe environments

## Life Skills Training

We prepare individuals for greater independence through practical life skills training:

- Daily living skills
- Money management
- Time management
- Transportation skills
- Health and wellness

## Family Support

We recognize that supporting an individual with autism means supporting the whole family. Our family programs include:

- Parent education and training
- Sibling support groups
- Family counseling
- Resource navigation
- Respite services

## Program Formats

Our programs are offered in various formats to meet different needs:

- Individual sessions
- Small group activities
- Community-based programs
- Virtual support options
- Weekend and evening programs
