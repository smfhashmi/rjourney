# Advocacy Services

Advocacy is about ensuring that individuals with autism and special needs have their voices heard, their rights protected, and their needs met. At R Journey, we provide comprehensive advocacy services to empower individuals and families.

## Our Advocacy Philosophy

We believe that:

- Every individual has the right to be heard
- Self-advocacy is a critical skill
- Families are powerful advocates
- Systems should work for people, not against them
- Change happens when voices unite

## Individual Advocacy

### Self-Advocacy Training

We teach individuals to:

- Understand their rights
- Express their needs and preferences
- Make informed decisions
- Navigate systems independently
- Build confidence in speaking up
- Develop self-advocacy skills

### Supported Decision-Making

For individuals who need support:

- Understanding options and choices
- Expressing preferences
- Making decisions with support
- Building decision-making skills
- Respecting autonomy

## Family Advocacy Support

### Education Rights Advocacy

Supporting families in educational settings:

- Understanding special education laws
- Participating in IEP meetings
- Advocating for appropriate services
- Resolving disputes
- Ensuring educational rights

### Healthcare Advocacy

Navigating healthcare systems:

- Understanding healthcare rights
- Communicating with providers
- Accessing appropriate care
- Coordinating services
- Managing insurance

### Service System Advocacy

Navigating disability service systems:

- Understanding eligibility and services
- Accessing needed supports
- Resolving service issues
- Coordinating multiple services
- Ensuring quality services
