# How to Help

Your support makes a real difference in the lives of individuals with autism and special needs and their families. There are many ways to get involved with R Journey and help us build bright futures together.

## Make a Donation

### One-Time Donations

Your financial contribution directly supports our programs and services:

- [Donate Online](https://link.clover.com/urlshortener/FQCNzr)
- Mail a check to our office
- Scan our QR code for quick mobile donations
- Corporate donations and sponsorships

### Monthly Giving

Become a monthly supporter and provide consistent, reliable funding:

- Set up automatic monthly donations
- Choose an amount that works for you
- Make a lasting impact with recurring support
- Easy to modify or cancel anytime

### Memorial and Tribute Gifts

Honor someone special:

- Memorial gifts in memory of a loved one
- Tribute gifts to celebrate special occasions
- Honor someone who has made a difference
- Recognition in our annual report

### Planned Giving

Leave a lasting legacy:

- Include R Journey in your will
- Designate R Journey as a beneficiary
- Create a special needs trust
- Consult with your financial advisor
