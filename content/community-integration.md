# Community Integration Programs

True inclusion means full participation in community life. At R Journey, we create opportunities for individuals with autism and special needs to engage with their communities, build relationships, and contribute their unique gifts.

## Our Vision for Community Integration

We believe that:

- Everyone belongs in their community
- Communities are stronger when everyone participates
- Relationships and connections enrich everyone's lives
- Community participation builds confidence and skills
- Inclusion benefits the entire community
