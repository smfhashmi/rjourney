Contact Us

We’re here to help! Whether you’re looking for information about our programs, need support, want to volunteer, or have questions, we’d love to hear from you.

Response Times

We strive to respond to all inquiries promptly:

  • Email inquiries: Within 24-48 hours
  • Urgent matters: Same day when possible
  • Program enrollment: Within 1 week
  • Volunteer applications: Within 1 week
  • Partnership inquiries: Within 1 week

After Hours and Emergencies

For urgent matters or emergencies outside of business hours, please:

  • Contact emergency services (911) for immediate emergencies
  • Reach out via email for non-urgent matters
  • We’ll respond as soon as possible during business hours
